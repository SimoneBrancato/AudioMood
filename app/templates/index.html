How to use

  • ★ Choose a model size for audio to text transcription:
    • Tiny (1GB VRAM)
    • Base (2GB VRAM)
    • Small (2GB VRAM)
    • Medium (4GB VRAM)
  • ★ Paste a YouTube URL and click Submit
  • ★ Wait a couple of minutes...
  • ★ Enjoy your sentiment analysis, tags recognition and summarization on your video!

AudioMood Features

  • ★ Summarize video content
  • ★ Sentiment analysis:
    • Evaluate each phrase as positive, neutral or negative
    • Check sentiment over time
  • ★ Tags recognition:
    • Top 10 tags by count
    • Check Top 5 tags position on video
